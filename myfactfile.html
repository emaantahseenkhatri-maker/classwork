All About Penguins

Penguins are flightless birds that live in the Southern Hemisphere.

Penguins are flightless seabirds that spend up to 75% of their lives in the ocean.

Penguins are excellent swimmers and can dive to depths of over 500 meters.

Penguins have a streamlined body shape that makes them efficient swimmers.
